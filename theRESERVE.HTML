About The Reserve

Nestled in the pristine wilderness of the Ochoco Mountains, The Reserve offers an unparalleled living experience where luxury meets nature. Our exclusive community spans over 500 acres of untouched forest, meadows, and scenic vistas.

Founded in 2018 by the visionary Henderson family, The Reserve was created with a commitment to preserving the natural beauty of the land while providing residents with sophisticated, modern living spaces.

Each property is thoughtfully designed to blend seamlessly with the surrounding landscape, utilizing sustainable materials and energy-efficient systems that minimize environmental impact while maximizing comfort and elegance.
